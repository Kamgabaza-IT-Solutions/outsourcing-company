About Us

Why We Built OutsourceSA

We saw a gap — brilliant South African professionals ready to work, and U.S. companies struggling with rising staffing costs. We built the bridge.

OutsourceSA helps American companies build high-performing remote teams in South Africa. We handle the sourcing, vetting, and matching — so you can focus on running your business.

Our mission is simple: make global talent accessible, affordable, and easy to manage. South Africa's combination of strong education, business experience, and competitive costs makes it one of the world's most exciting outsourcing destinations.

Major cities like Cape Town, Johannesburg, and Durban host thriving technology and outsourcing sectors with deep talent pools and world-class infrastructure.
